Review of Baseus MC10 Earbud

July 20, 2025 Review

A practical look at comfort, sound quality, calls, battery life, and whether the MC10 is worth buying.

Comfort

The MC10 is light enough for long sessions and stays secure for casual walking. The fit may need tip-swapping if you want stronger passive isolation.

Sound and Calls

The tuning is energetic, with enough bass for everyday listening. Calls are usable indoors, though busy outdoor environments still expose the microphones.

Battery

Battery life is solid for the price, and the case gives enough top-ups for a normal week of commuting and desk listening.

Bottom line: A sensible budget earbud if you value comfort and price over premium noise cancellation.
