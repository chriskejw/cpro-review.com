Productivity Hacks for Developers

Published on Aug 17, 2025

Being productive while learning or working as a developer is crucial. Some techniques that helped me:

  • Pomodoro technique (25 min focused work + 5 min break)
  • Keeping a simple to-do list and prioritizing tasks
  • Using keyboard shortcuts to speed up repetitive actions

Small habits compound over time. Consistency is better than long sporadic bursts.
