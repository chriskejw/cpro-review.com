Top 5 Tech Accessories 2025

August 14, 2025 Accessories

We tested dozens of accessories this year. These five deliver the biggest quality-of-life improvements for most people.

  1. 1) Compact USB-C Hub

    HDMI 2.1, 100W passthrough, and two USB-A ports in a pocketable slab. It runs cool and never dropped a connection in our tests.

  2. 2) Wireless Ergonomic Mouse

    Excellent sensor, silent clicks, and 3 devices paired. Battery life exceeded the manufacturer rating by 15% in our use.

  3. 3) Foldable Laptop Stand

    Light yet rigid. Improves typing angle and airflow; folds flat into a sleeve pocket.

  4. 4) Portable SSD (USB 3.2 Gen 2x2)

    Real-world transfers hit ~1.9GB/s. Includes a short, durable cable and rubber sleeve.

  5. 5) Magnetic Cable Clips

    Keep your desk tidy and cables accessible. Doesn’t leave residue on most surfaces.

How We Chose

We prioritize reliability and repeatable test results over spec sheets. Each item was daily-driven for at least a week by multiple reviewers.

Bottom line: Start with the hub and mouse; add the SSD if you move big files. The rest depends on your setup.
