Top 3 iPhone Accessories

August 7, 2025 Accessory

Three small upgrades that make charging, carrying, and using your iPhone easier every day.

  1. 1. Compact MagSafe Stand

    A stable stand keeps notifications visible, works well on a desk, and doubles as a simple nightstand charger.

  2. 2. Braided USB-C Cable

    A good cable is boring in the best way: durable, fast enough for daily use, and easy to spot in a bag.

  3. 3. Slim Card Grip

    A grip with one or two cards can replace a wallet for quick trips without adding much bulk.

Bottom line: Start with the cable, add the stand if you charge at a desk, and pick the grip only if you want fewer things in your pocket.
